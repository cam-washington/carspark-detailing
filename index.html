[03] / What We Do

Mobile detailing services in Sparks & Reno

Two straightforward services, fixed pricing, and everything done in your driveway. No packages to decode and no upsells.

Full Interior & Exterior Detail

From $170

Our signature service is a complete, single-visit detail that covers the whole vehicle inside and out. Inside, we clear the trash, vacuum every surface, shampoo the seats and carpets, wipe down the cabin, clean the interior glass and condition the trim. Outside, we pressure wash, run a shampoo foam wash, scrub the wheels and wheel wells, dress the tires, clean the exterior windows, lay down a spray wax and finish with a scratch-free dry. It's the same process whether you drive a daily commuter or a weekend car — nothing gets skipped. Because we're mobile, all of it happens in your driveway in Sparks or Reno while you go about your day.

$170 sedan & coupe · $190 SUV & truck · Add-ons: engine bay clean $40 · headlight restoration $75

Maintenance Detail

$120 / visit

Once your car has had a full detail, a maintenance detail keeps it there without starting from scratch every time. On a monthly or bi-monthly visit we remove trash and vacuum the full interior, wipe everything down, then pressure wash and shampoo-foam the exterior, clean the exterior windows and finish with a scratch-free dry. It's a lighter, faster service built for upkeep rather than deep restoration — the easiest way to keep that fresh-detail feeling between full details. It's ideal for busy commuters and leased vehicles, or anyone who just likes their car looking cared-for all year. Like everything we do, it happens at your home or office anywhere in Sparks, Reno and Washoe County.

$120 per visit on a recurring monthly or bi-monthly schedule

[07] / Good To Know

Frequently asked questions

Do you come to me, or do I drop my car off?

We're fully mobile — we come to you. We set up and detail your vehicle right in your driveway or parking spot anywhere in Sparks, Reno and the surrounding Washoe County area. There's no drop-off; you can stay home or at work while we handle it.

What areas do you serve?

Sparks and Reno, NV, plus the surrounding Washoe County communities. If you're not sure whether you're in range, text us your address and we'll confirm.

How long does a full detail take?

A full interior and exterior detail usually takes about 2 to 4 hours, depending on the size of the vehicle and how much cleanup it needs. Trucks, SUVs and heavily soiled interiors take longer. We'd rather take the time to do it right than rush and cut corners.

How much does a detail cost?

Pricing is fixed, so there's never a wait on a quote. A full interior and exterior detail is $170 for sedans and coupes, or $190 for SUVs and trucks. A recurring maintenance detail is $120 on a monthly or bi-monthly schedule. Add-ons: engine bay clean $40, headlight restoration $75.

Do I need to provide anything?

Just two things: access to a water hose and a power outlet. Because we come to you, we tap into those on-site — we bring all the products, tools and equipment ourselves.

Do you offer paint correction or ceramic coating?

Not at this time. We focus on doing full interior and exterior details extremely well rather than spreading into services we can't stand behind. If that changes, it'll be listed here first.

How do I book, and when are you available?

Text or email us with your vehicle and where you're located. We run by appointment seven days a week and will get you on the schedule at a time that works for you.
